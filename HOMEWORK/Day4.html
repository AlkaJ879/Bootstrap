FAQ


How can I check if my Apple product is still under warranty?
You can easily check your warranty status by visiting Apple’s official Check Coverage page. Just enter your product’s serial number, and you’ll see details about your warranty and support eligibility.
Can I use the same Apple ID on multiple devices?
Yes! You can use the same Apple ID across all your Apple devices — including iPhone, iPad, Mac, and Apple Watch. This allows you to sync data, access purchases, and use services like iCloud and iMessage seamlessly across all your devices.
